History and Facts

Shri Kedarnath Dham

One of the four important sites of Shri Kedarnath Dham is in the Garhwal Himalayan range near the Mandakini river in Uttarakhand. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the most revered pilgrimage places in India.

The temple is open during the yatra season and attracts pilgrims from across the country. Due to high altitude and weather changes, passengers must keep identity documents, registration proof, and ticket details ready for verification.

Shri Badrinath Dham

The temple of Shri Badrinath Dham is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is situated in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand. It is one of the most important temples in the Char Dham route and is visited by thousands of pilgrims each season.

Correct passenger details are required for booking, payment confirmation, and further communication. Passengers should follow reporting time, document rules, and helipad instructions carefully.
